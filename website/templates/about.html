Welcome to the Grocery App

The dataset used for this project can be found on Kaggle: Grocery Inventory Dataset .

In our dataset we work with five variables:

  • Product_Name – Categorical, nominal. Represents the name of the product.
  • Category – Categorical, nominal. Type or category of the product.
  • Supplier_Name – Categorical, nominal. The company or supplier that provides the product.
  • Stock_Quantity – Numerical, discrete. Number of items in stock.
  • Unit_Price – Numerical, continuous. Price per unit of the product.
